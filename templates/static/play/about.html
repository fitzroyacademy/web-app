We make videos.

Fast facts on our video production:

  • It takes anywhere from 100-200 hours of work to make a single lesson.
  • All our camera gear fits in one pelican case, and is pretty simple stuff.
  • So far we've filmed in Melbourne, Sydney, London, Denpasar, San Francisco, Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, and Puerto Galera. We'd like to go to Nairobi.
  • We've produced over 870 minutes of content. That's ~15 hours, or if you watch one lesson per week, or 6+ months of continuous learning!

Eve Byers (alumni, we miss you!) and Leo Flander do all the hard work. They're award winning film makers and artists in their own right.
