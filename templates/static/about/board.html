Bored reports. Suit up, it's serious business time.

Q: What's all this then?

We have been making video board reports each quarter for our geographically challenged yet lovely and intellgent advisory board.

We're putting them up here for everyone to see as another step in our public accountability, alongside the mistakes page.

Q: What can the public see?

You get to see the videos, and links to as many of our internal documents as we can get away with sharing publicly. That means plans, policies.

Stuff like our financials, who we hire, and any sensitive internal stuff won't be shared here.

Q: Has this been done before?

Yes! We were inspired by other companies, including Watsi and their radical transparency approach, and PledgeMe and Chuffed.

Ha ha, business! Newest first.
